# 🌞 Karan Agent Hub - User Guide

Welcome to the **Karan Agent Hub**, your central platform for managing solar leads, tracking commissions, and building your sales network. This guide will help you understand how to navigate and utilize the system effectively.

---

## 1. Getting Started

### Roles & Permissions
The platform uses a role-based system to determine what you can see and do:
- **Admin**: Has full access to configure products, approve agents, and oversee all deals and payouts.
- **Salesperson (Internal)**: Can add leads, manage deals, generate quotations, and view internal analytics.
- **Sales Agent**: An independent partner who can refer sub-agents, log leads, and track their downline commission payouts.
- **Sub Sales Agent**: A partner working under a Sales Agent. They can log their own leads and view their own commissions.

### Logging In
1. Go to the login page.
2. Enter your email and password.
3. If you have forgotten your password, contact an Admin for a reset link.

---

## 2. Managing Leads & Deals

### Adding a New Lead
1. Navigate to **Leads** in the left sidebar menu.
2. Click the **"Add Lead"** button.
3. Fill out the candidate's basic information (Name, Phone, Email, Location).
4. Save the lead. The lead will now appear in your active pipeline.

### Converting Leads to Deals
When a Lead is ready to receive a quotation and move forward:
1. Open the Lead's detail page.
2. Click **"Convert to Deal"**.
3. The lead will disappear from the Leads board and appear in the **Deals** board.
4. You can now track the Deal's status (e.g., Quotation Sent, Negotiation, Closed Won).

---

## 3. Generating Quotations

The platform features an automated Bill of Materials (BOM) calculator for generating professional quotes.

1. Go to a Deal's detail page.
2. Click the **"Quotation"** tab.
3. **Select System Type**: Choose between On-grid, Off-grid, Hybrid, or VFD/Drive. The system will automatically populate standard components for that system type.
4. **Input Capacity**: Enter the desired kW capacity. The system will automatically multiply the base component costs by the capacity.
5. **Adjust Margins**: Add your desired profit margin percentage.
6. **Generate Document**: Click **"Generate PDF Quotation"**. You can print this, download it, or send it directly to the client's email via the built-in system.

---

## 4. Hierarchy & Network (Agent Structure)

If you are a Sales Agent, you can recruit Sub Sales Agents to work under you and earn a percentage of their closed deals.

### Viewing the Network Map
1. Click on **"Network"** in the sidebar.
2. **Interactive Graph**: A draggable, zoomable visual map of all agents. It shows who reports to whom, their total closed deals, and their roles.
3. **Tree View**: A simple hierarchical list that expands downwards.

### Inviting a Sub-Agent
1. Go to the **Agents** page.
2. Click on the **"Referral Link"** button on your profile card to copy your unique invite link.
3. Send this link to a prospect. When they fill out the form, your **Agent Code** will automatically be filled in as the "Referrer Code."
4. Once an Admin approves their application, they will appear under you in the Network Map.

---

## 5. Commissions

Commissions are automatically calculated when a Deal is marked as **Closed Won**. 

- **Fixed Amount**: A flat rupee rate paid per closed deal.
- **Percentage**: A percentage based on the final deal value.
- **Downline Commissions**: Sales Agents receive an automated cut from their Sub Sales Agent's closed deals.

To view your earnings, go to **Cash / Commissions** in the sidebar. You can filter by month, view pending payouts, and download your earning statements.
